The problem I'd been wrestling with for days suddenly seemed clearer in the morning light. Sometimes the best debugging happens not at your desk, but in these quiet in-between moments.

I wrote the first line of code, then another. The solution unfolded naturally, like it had been there all along, waiting for me to stop overthinking and just start typing.
